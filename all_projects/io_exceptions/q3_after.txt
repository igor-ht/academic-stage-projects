Donald John Trump (born June 14 1946) is 45th current President United States office since January 20 2017 Before entering politics was a businessman television personality
Trump was born New York City borough Queens He earned an economics degree from Wharton School University Pennsylvania A third-generation businessman Trump followed footsteps his grandmother Elizabeth father Fred running family real estate company He served as chairman president TTrump Organization from 1971 until January 2017 Upon his inauguration as president Trump delegated company management to his sons Donald Jr Eric Trumps business career primarily focused on building or renovating office towers hotels casinos golf courses
Trump also started several side ventures branded various products with his name He is credited as having written or co-written several books (including TArt Deal) produced hosted TApprentice television series for 12 years As 2017 was 544th richest person world with an estimated net worth $35 billion
Trump had long expressed interest politics He entered 2016 presidential race as a Republican defeated sixteen opponents primaries Commentators described his political positions as populist protectionist nationalist His campaign received extensive free media coverage many his public statements were controversial or false Trump won general election on November 8 2016 a surprise victory against Democratic opponent Hillary Clinton He became oldest (by time inauguration) wealthiest person ever to assume presidency first without prior military or government service fifth to have won election despite losing popular vote His election policies have sparked numerous protests
In domestic policy Trump has unsuccessfully attempted to repeal replace Affordable Care Act He appointed Neil Gorsuch to Supreme Court He ordered a travel ban on citizens from six Muslim-majority countries citing security concerns ban was partially implemented after several legal challenges In foreign policy withdrew United States from Trans-Pacific Partnership Paris Climate Agreement undid parts Cuban Thaw ordered missile strikes Syria response to Khan Shaykhun chemical attack After Trump dismissed FBI Director James Comey Justice Department appointed his predecessor Robert Mueller as special counsel to investigate Russias interference presidential election potential links between Russia Trump campaign associates any related matters
